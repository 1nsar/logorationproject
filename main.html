About us

Welcome to our organization, where our mission is to support children with speech disorders through the development of a mobile app.

We understand the difficulties and challenges that families with children who have speech disorders face on a daily basis. That is why our team has worked tirelessly to create a cutting-edge mobile app that can aid in the communication and speech development of these children.
